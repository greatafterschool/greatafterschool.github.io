Great After School (GAS)

GAS is all about enabling fantastic education and family activities that occur beyond the traditional school environment. It is an umbrella (to be 501c3) non-profit that sponsors:

  • http://PaloBots.org – A club that helps kids learn about robotics, programming technology, and teamwork.
  • http://MinimalBuyableBook.com – A program that teaches kids how to publish.
  • http://InsanelyGreatTennis.com – A program that helps kids and adults play tennis at an awesome new level
  • GAS Robotics – A spin-off from http://www.firstLegoLeague.org aiming to reduce the friction of participating in an awesome robotics and learning culture
  • Several other projects
